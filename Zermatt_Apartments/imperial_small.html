Haus Imperial Studio

Haus Imperial is in a quiet but very central location, less than a 5 minute walk from the railway station, the departure stations for Sunnegga and Gornergrat, and the ski bus to the Matterhorn Paradise departure station. The Bahnhostrasse which is the heart of the village and where you will find the best restaurants, bars and shops is just a 3 minute walk away.
A 38m2 non-smoking studio right next door to the Imperial apartment. Comprises double bed (160cm), living area, kitchen area (magnificent Matterhorn view from the dining table and living area), shower/WC bathroom. Fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher, microwave, cooker with oven, capsule coffee maker, and everything you need to make fondu and raclette. Large balcony with Matterhorn view.
